Spicy Beef Bulgogi

Description

Beef Bulgogi is a Korean dish that consists of marinaded and fried flank steak with peppers and onions. This dish is often served with white rice and a combination of Korean side dishes. I am a huge fan of this dish, and usually alternate between it and spicy pork when I am eating Korean food.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup siracha sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons whole dried cayenne peppers
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper, or to tatse
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place flank steak slices in a shallow dish.
  2. Mix soy sauce, sriracha sauce, green onion, sugar, whole dried peppers, garlic, sesame seeds, sesame oil, ground cayenne, and black pepper together in a bowl. Pour over steak. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate to marinate, at least 1 hour but preferably overnight.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Remove steak slices from marinade and grill on preheated grill until slightly charred and cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
  5. Simmer marinade in a wok or skillet over medium heat until thick and the consistency of gravy; pour over grilled steak.
